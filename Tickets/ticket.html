INTRODUCE

All people love anime

Anime (アニメ) is the way to call animated movies produced from manga series (comics) in Japan. Therefore, when it comes to anime, people will immediately think of Japanese cartoons.

Initially, the people in the animation production team used the English word animation to refer to the movies. However, the audience uses other phrases such as manga movie, TV manga, ... to name them because it resembles moving manga images. This many people consider it too troublesome and does not convey all the messages, so the term anime became a phrase when referring to Japanese animated films.
